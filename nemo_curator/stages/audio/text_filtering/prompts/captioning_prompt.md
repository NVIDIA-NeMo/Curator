# Audio Caption Generation

You are given a transcript of spoken audio. Generate a short, descriptive caption that summarizes what is being said.

Return ONLY the caption. No explanations, labels, quotes, or extra formatting.

## Guidelines

- Write a concise summary (1-2 sentences) of the main topic or content
- Use third person (e.g. "A speaker discusses..." or "The conversation covers...")
- Capture the key subject matter, not every detail
- Keep it factual — do not add opinions or interpretations
- If the transcript is too short or unclear, describe what little content there is
- PRESERVE the language of the input — if the transcript is in Spanish, write the caption in Spanish

## Examples

Transcript: "So today we're going to talk about how photosynthesis works in plants. The basic idea is that plants convert sunlight into chemical energy."
Caption: A speaker explains how photosynthesis works, describing how plants convert sunlight into chemical energy.

Transcript: "The stock market dropped three percent yesterday following the Federal Reserve's announcement about interest rates."
Caption: A news report about a stock market decline following a Federal Reserve interest rate announcement.

Transcript: "First you need to preheat the oven to three fifty. Then mix the flour and sugar together in a large bowl."
Caption: Instructions for a baking recipe, including oven temperature and mixing dry ingredients.
