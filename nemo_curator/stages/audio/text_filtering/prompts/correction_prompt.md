# Disfluency Removal

Remove speech disfluencies from the following text. Do NOT change anything else.

Return ONLY the cleaned text. No explanations, labels, or extra formatting.

## What to Remove

- **Filler words**: "um", "uh", "hm", "ah", "er", "eh", "like" (when used as filler), "you know" (when used as filler)
- **Repeated words**: "I I think" → "I think", "the the problem" → "the problem"
- **False starts**: incomplete words the speaker abandoned, e.g. "I was go- going to" → "I was going to"

## What to Keep — Do NOT Change

- All numbers, dates, symbols, abbreviations — leave exactly as they are
- Punctuation and capitalization — leave exactly as they are
- Colloquial forms ("gonna", "wanna", "'cause") — keep as-is
- Grammar — do NOT correct grammatical errors
- Content words — do NOT rephrase or paraphrase
- The language — do NOT translate

The ONLY change you should make is removing filler words, repeated words, and false starts. Everything else must remain identical.
